About Our Company

NSS is a Risk Management company protecting of human information and physical infrastructure.

NSS offers a range of solutions that spanning the entire risk spectrum.

  • Information security
  • Physical risk assessment
  • Business continuity
  • Compliance assessments
  • Digital forensics
  • Social media monitoring
  • Protective services
  • Travel advisory
  • Brand protection
  • IPR investigation

NSS has worked with sensitive Indian and international government agencies to develop National ICT security framework and policies.

NSS created India's Centre for Excellence in Information and Network Security (CINS), conceptualized and set up the Indian Computer Emergency Response Team (CERT-In), trained law enforcement officers, assisted police forces in matters of cyber crime and online fraud investigations. NSS has also handled key assignments for international governments & Fortune 500 companies. NSS is present in India, Malaysia, Singapore and UAE.
