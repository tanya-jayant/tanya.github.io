About Our Company

Network Security Solutions (I) Ltd (www.mynetsec.com), offering a full range of risk solutions such as Security Consulting, Brand Protection, IPR Investigation, Information Security and Customized Solutions to Government, Quasi-Government and private worldwide. NSS is instrumental in creating India's National level Centre for Excellence in Information and Network Security (CINS), Conceptualized and Setup the Indian Computer Emergency Response Team (CERT-In), trained law enforcement officers, assisted police forces in Cyber Crime and Online fraud investigations apart from handling key assignments for International Governments & fortune 500 companiesis headquartered out of Delhi with offices in Pune and Kuala Lumpur (Malaysia).

Over the last 17 years, NSS has developed into one of the largest ‘Information Assurance Companies’’ in the region with over 100 corporate and 30 government clients. NSS has worked with sensitive Indian and International Government departments to help nations develop their ICT framework and audit policies. We have provided turnkey solutions ensuring e-defence capabilities on national infrastructure for both Indian and foreign governments.

Our key differentiators could be APAC’s leading risk management and consulting company. Vastly experienced resources in investigations offering a complete suite of pure play risk consulting services across the industry such as InfoSec, entertainment, FMCG, Telecom and government organizations. We regularly carry out risk assessments and implementation projects both for Governments as well as private companies.

NSS quality focus & core competence in information security has enabled us to offer our clients best of breed information security consulting services, product development, research, training and IS management solutions. A preferred risk management partner for a number of local and multinational companies where we provide onsite and offsite vendor compliance management services. We are proud to have our client base rooted in both Private and Public Sectors.
