SOCIAL MEDIA MONITORING

Internet is powerful platform for expressing our ideologies but sometimes what is being posted on social media, can affect an organization and brand value can suffer because of this. NSS offers to its clients a complete monitoring of social media to mitigate the reduction of a brand value.

Social media monitoring is the act of using a tool to listen to what is being said across the internet; monitoring media not just from traditional publishers, but on millions of social sites too.

The listening spots or the platforms crawled are :

  • Forums
  • Blogs
  • Review Sites
  • News Sites
  • RSS Feeds

Major Social Networks: Facebook, Twitter, Google+, Pinterest, YouTube, LinkedIn, etc.

NSS Methodology

  1. See keywords relating to queries to see which words are trending
  2. Track consumer sentiments about a particular topic (Sentiment analysis)
  3. Observe engagement trends (when are people talking about brand)
  4. Identify key influencers talking about the brand online
  5. Compare/benchmark statistics gathered from the above information
  6. Create customisable dashboards with many different kinds of graphs
  7. Reporting of the analysis
