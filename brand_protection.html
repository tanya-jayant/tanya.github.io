BRAND PROTECTION

Brand Protection reposes the faith customers have in the brand and also helps brands to focus their energies.

NSS is possibly one of a unique provider of Brand Protection that has the capability to monitor and protect Brands in both online and offline space. Already in use by a few of the Fortune 100, these service solutions focus on key areas of brand management and protection, providing prioritized, actionable brand protection intelligence.

NSS understands that brand equity is one of the most important assets of any company and we recommend a holistic methodology to brand protection. Designed to enable companies to protect revenue, reputation and customer trust online, all NSS brand protection solutions leverage unmatched access to data, plus unparalleled capabilities for prevention, detection and response. While all are integrated, each is fully self-contained, enabling enterprises to add new capabilities as business needs dictate.



Brand Protection Services


NSS offers brand protection services in the context of their Brand when a client has one of the following problems:

  • When the client has doubts over its supply chain for not working according to the rules and regulations of the organization and illegal buying or selling activities.
  • When the client is suspicious, has information about counterfeiting of its brands being sold from retail outlets.
  • Conducting raids on manufacturing sites of counterfeit labels, used products being used in makeshifts factories/establishments.
