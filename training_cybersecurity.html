RANSOMWARE

Ransomware is computer malware that installs covertly on a victim's computer, executes a crypto virology attack that adversely affects it, and demands a ransom payment to decrypt it or not publish it. Simple ransomware may lock the system in a way which is not difficult for a knowledgeable person to reverse, and display a message requesting payment to unlock it. Thus, ransomware is a denial-of-access attack that prevents computer users from accessing files since it is intractable to decrypt the files without the decryption key.

Objectives

  • What is ransomware (Cause and Prevention)
  • Bit coin (Link between Ransomware and Bit coins)
  • Types of Ransomware
  • Impact and recommendations by our experts.

ACTIVE SHOOTING

Active shooting and lone wolf terrorism is an increasing epidemic. Every year, world over, thousands of people die or get injured due to active shooter events. Motives of the active shooters who are involved in these events can range from terrorism, radical ideologies to growing anger, domestic disputes etc.

Objectives

  • To gain more awareness on an active shooter and active shooting incident.
  • To prepare more effective strategies for emergency situations and for better coordination with the law & enforcement

Description

  • What is an Active Shooter Situation and how to identify the early signs
  • Types of Active Shooters, their Psychological Profile and Motives
  • Preparation for an Active Shooter situation- Before an attack
  • Defensive and Offensive measures when under attack
  • How and what to expect from Law Enforcement
  • Handling the Aftermath- Casualties and fear
  • Lessons for CSOs and facility staff
  • Case study and demo drills

WOMEN'S SAFETY AND SECURITY

As India races towards becoming an economic superpower, workplace has become a much more diverse environment. Women now represent 25% of the total workforce in India, their personal safety and security is becoming the central issue with the corporate houses. To ensure maximum productivity at workplace, it is important to give preference to Gender Equality and a safe working environment for women.

Physical Security focuses on the physical security of the women employees while they are on jobs or inside the workplace and in transit.
Environmental Security compliments the physical safety. Also it focuses on the gender equality and institutional policies like zero tolerance policy on sexual harassment at workplace.
Organizational Security is creating a positive and hastlefree environment at the workplace where women feel secure and safe and encouraged to put in her maximum effort with dignity and respect. It is the duty of the employees and the organization in general.
Educational Security focuses on making the women employees aware of the companies policy on Gender Discriminatio and Sexual harassment. They should also be encouraged to report such instances without fear. Regular training on security and safety should be provided.
