TRAININGS ON CYBERSECURITY AND RANSOMWARE

Cyber hacking and the breach of information systems security is an emerging threat to most companies in the world, tipped as one of the top risks in the sector. As a risk mitigation service and awareness to crime, NSS conducts basic cybersecurity workshop.

OBJECTIVES OF THE Course

NSS provides Protective Services to both the Corporate and Personal sectors.

  • Close Protection Services
  • Specialist Security Services
  • Residential Protection
  • Valuables & Asset Escort & Delivery
  • Child & Adult Recovery
  • Special Event Security
  • Halls and walls in the hotel

Modus Operandi

  • Close protection agents will carry out reconnaissance of the area of operations a day prior.
  • The agents shall be embedded with the representative from the point of arrival at airport.
  • CPO and Halls and Walls agents thereafter will travel and stay with the representative and be available at all times.
  • On arrival at the destination the teams will be closely protecting the representative and will have contingency plans for each eventuality.
  • The CPO agent will be accompanying the executive on his/her way back to the airport after completion of the project.
  • The teams will plan their exit and ensure total safety of the representative.
